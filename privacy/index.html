Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

CityTimeHub ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use our website (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

CityTimeHub does not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for personal information such as your name or address. Most of our features run entirely in your browser. We may collect limited, non-personal information automatically, such as browser type, device type, general location (country/region), and pages visited, for analytics and performance purposes.

2. Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to remember your preferences (such as your theme choice and recent comparisons). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

3. Cookies

We and our partners use cookies and similar technologies to operate the Service, understand usage, and display advertising. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended.

4. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Service. Google Analytics collects information such as pages visited and time spent, using cookies. This helps us improve the Service. You can learn more in the Google Privacy & Terms.

5. Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display ads.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • For more information about how Google uses data, visit How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights over your personal data, including access, correction, and deletion. For visitors in the EEA, where required, we (and our partners) obtain consent for the use of non-essential cookies through a consent banner before such cookies are set.

7. Third-Party Services

The Service relies on trusted third-party data providers (for example, for prayer times and sunrise/sunset data). These providers may receive your approximate location to return relevant results. We do not control their privacy practices.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date reflects the latest revision.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@citytimehub.com.
