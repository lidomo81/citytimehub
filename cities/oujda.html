00 — Overview

Time & prayer in Oujda

Local time in Oujda, Morocco, runs on the Africa/Casablanca time zone (UTC+1). The clock and dates at the top of this page are computed live for that zone, so they stay correct for Oujda wherever in the world you happen to be reading from. Where daylight saving time applies, the offset can move by an hour between summer and winter.

Sitting at a latitude of about 34.7° in the northern hemisphere, Oujda's day length varies clearly between the long days of summer and the shorter days of winter. That is why its sunrise, sunset and day-length figures change from month to month, and why prayer times here follow the position of the sun rather than a fixed clock.

The prayer times shown for Oujda are calculated using the Egyptian General Authority of Survey. The five daily prayers track the sun, so each one drifts a little earlier or later as the seasons turn. For the full picture you can open the monthly timetable, the live prayer clock, or the Qibla direction for Oujda from the tools on this site.
