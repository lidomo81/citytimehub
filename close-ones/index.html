      How Close Ones works

      Add mom, dad, a sibling, or anyone — in the same city or thousands of miles away. Pick a quick template or build your own rule: after a prayer (call 30 minutes after Fajr), before a prayer (nudge 15 minutes before Fajr until adhan), or a fixed appointment on their local Tuesday at 10:00.

      CityTimeHub converts every window to your clock so you know when to act. Optional WhatsApp opens with your message ready. Everything stays on your device — no accounts, no cloud.

      Frequently Asked Questions

      Does it work in the same city?

      Yes. If you and your parent live in the same city, rules still anchor to prayer times and local habits — wake after Fajr, nudge before Fajr, Friday calls, and fixed appointments.

      Who gets the notification?

      You do. The tool tells you when to call or message. It does not push to their phone unless they use the app themselves.

      Where do prayer times come from?

      AlAdhan API using each city's coordinates — the same source used across CityTimeHub.
