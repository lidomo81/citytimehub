About Us

Welcome to CityTimeHub, your ultimate digital companion for tracking global time, daylight movement, and precise prayer times across the globe.

In today's hyper-connected world, time zones should bring us together, not hold us back. That's why we built a seamless, visually striking platform that gives you an instant, live view of world clocks, time differences, and astronomical data — all in one place.

Our Mission

We believe that "It is always Now… somewhere on Earth." Our goal is to empower remote teams, global travelers, digital nomads, and practicing Muslims by providing real-time, highly accurate time and prayer data through a fast, lightweight, and clutter-free interface.

What Makes Us Different?

  • Instant Client-Side Calculations: Global times and UTC differences are computed directly in your browser for lightning-fast results.
  • Live Day & Night Indicator: See who's awake and who's asleep across cities at a glance.
  • Smart Time-Zone Comparison: Overlap working hours between any two cities with a visual meeting planner.
  • Accurate Prayer Times & Astronomical Data: Daily prayer schedules with Hijri/Gregorian dates, plus sunrise and sunset times.
  • Privacy & Reliability First: Your experience and privacy are our top priorities. CityTimeHub is completely free, optimized for mobile, and relies on trusted, established data sources for maximum accuracy.

Who CityTimeHub Is For

The people who reach for CityTimeHub usually share one thing: their day is tangled up with more than one place. A developer on a distributed team trying to find an hour that isn't midnight for half the group. A parent watching their child study abroad, doing the mental arithmetic of "is it too late to call right now?" A traveller who just landed and wants to know when the sun sets and when the next prayer falls in a city they've never been to. A Muslim far from home who simply wants prayer times they can trust, wherever they happen to be. We built the site so that none of these moments require a spreadsheet or a second guess.

How We Calculate Time & Prayer

Wherever it can, CityTimeHub does the work in your own browser. World clocks and UTC differences are computed live on your device using the standard time-zone database, so the numbers update in real time and keep working even when your connection is poor. For prayer times we draw on the well-established AlAdhan service and apply the calculation method that is conventional for each city's region — the Egyptian General Authority of Survey for Cairo, Umm al-Qura for Makkah, the Muslim World League as a sensible default elsewhere, and so on. Sunrise, sunset and day-length figures come from astronomical data for the exact coordinates of each city. Because the five daily prayers follow the position of the sun rather than the clock, those times shift gently through the year — which is exactly why a live, location-aware tool is more useful than a fixed printed table.

Why We Built It

CityTimeHub started from a simple frustration: the tools for "what time is it there?" were either cluttered with ads and pop-ups, or buried prayer times three menus deep, or both. We wanted something that opens fast, shows you what you need at a glance, and treats your attention with respect. The guiding line we kept coming back to is that "it is always Now… somewhere on Earth" — time isn't a barrier between people, it's just a coordinate, and a good tool should make that coordinate effortless to read.

Free, Fast & Privacy-First

CityTimeHub is completely free to use, works on any phone or computer, and can be installed as an app that keeps running offline after its first load. We don't require an account, and we keep the experience clean and lightweight. You can read exactly how we handle data — including the cookies used to support advertising — on our Privacy Policy page, and you can always reach a real person through our Contact page.

Thank you for choosing CityTimeHub to help you make sense of time — yours, and everyone else's.
